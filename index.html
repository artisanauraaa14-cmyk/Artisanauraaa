About Us ✿

Handmade with chaos. Finished with love.

Hi, I'm Vaishnavi Reddy — a 16-year-old with too many hobbies and not enough hours in a day.

"Jack of all trades, master of none — but oftentimes better than a master of one." So I didn't stop. Ever. Dance, guitar, books, art — and then yarn. And I couldn't put it down.

What started as my own late-night creating grew into something bigger — a team in the making. Artisan Auraaa is now powered by a wonderful group of makers aged 16 to 60, each bringing their own magic to every piece.

Every piece is 100% handmade. You're not just buying something cute. You're supporting real people, real creativity, and very big dreams !!

And somewhere between the stitches and the sketchbooks, I started writing — and now that same energy goes into a magazine. Welcome to the chaos 🌸
